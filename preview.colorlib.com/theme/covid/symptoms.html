Symptoms of Coronavirus

Prompt recognition of symptoms is key to early intervention and reducing the spread of the virus.

High Fever

A significant increase in body temperature, often a prominent early sign of COVID-19. If you experience this symptom, consult a healthcare professional.

Cough

A persistent dry or productive cough is a common symptom of COVID-19. If you have a persistent cough, especially in combination with other symptoms, it's important to seek medical advice and get tested for COVID-19.

Sore Troath

A scratchy, irritated, or painful throat can be a symptom of COVID-19. If you experience a sore throat, it's advisable to monitor your health and consider getting tested for COVID-19.

Headache

Persistent headaches can also be a symptom of COVID-19. If you have an ongoing headache, particularly when accompanied by other symptoms, it's crucial to seek medical guidance and consider COVID-19 testing.

How To Protect Yourself

Equip yourself with knowledge, vigilance, and responsible actions to safeguard your health and the health of others.

You should do

  • Stay at home
  • Wear mask
  • Use Sanitizer
  • Disinfect your home
  • Wash your hands
  • Frequent self-isolation

You should avoid

  • Avoid infected people
  • Avoid animals
  • Avoid handshaking
  • Aviod infected surfaces
  • Don't touch your face
  • Avoid droplets
